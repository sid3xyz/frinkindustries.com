Superhuman Samurai Syber-Squad (SSSS) is the coolest show ever made! It started in 1994 and follows the adventures of Sam Collins and his friends.

The Plot

Sam Collins is a high schooler who gets sucked into the computer and becomes Servo. He fights mega-viruses created by the evil (but misunderstood?) Malcolm Frink.
