Denkou Choujin Gridman

Before Sam Collins, there was Naoto Sho. Released in 1993 by Tsuburaya Productions (the makers of Ultraman!), Gridman is the original "Computer World" hero.

Differences from SSSS

  • The Villain: In Gridman, the villain is Takeshi Todo. He is much darker than Malcolm Frink.
  • The Demon Lord: Instead of Kilokahn, we have Khan Digifer.
  • The Tone: Gridman is serious tokusatsu action!

Legacy

Gridman inspired the anime SSSS.GRIDMAN in 2018! It proves the Computer World never dies.
